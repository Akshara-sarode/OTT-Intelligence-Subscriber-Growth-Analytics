# 🎬 OTT Intelligence & Subscriber Growth Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

OTT Intelligence & Subscriber Growth Analytics is a comprehensive data analytics and business intelligence project focused on evaluating the strategic merger between **LioCinema** and **Jotstar**, two leading OTT streaming platforms in India.

Using SQL and Power BI, this project analyzes subscriber behavior, content consumption patterns, user engagement, subscription trends, and revenue performance to uncover actionable insights that support business growth and strategic decision-making.

The analysis helps stakeholders understand customer preferences, optimize content strategies, improve user retention, and maximize revenue opportunities for the merged streaming platform.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Lio plans to merge with Jotstar to establish a dominant presence in India's rapidly growing OTT market.

To ensure a successful merger strategy, leadership teams require data-driven insights into:

* Subscriber Growth Trends
* User Demographics
* Content Consumption Patterns
* Watch-Time Behavior
* Active vs Inactive Users
* Subscription Upgrade & Downgrade Trends
* Revenue Performance
* Platform Engagement Metrics

The objective is to identify growth opportunities, improve customer retention, and support long-term business expansion.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 👥 Subscriber Behavior

* Tier 1 cities contribute the highest share of premium subscribers.
* Younger audiences demonstrate stronger engagement and higher watch-time activity.
* Mobile devices dominate content consumption across both platforms.
* Active users significantly contribute to overall platform growth.

### 📈 Subscription Trends

* Free-to-Premium upgrades are highly common, indicating strong monetization opportunities.
* Inactive users exhibit higher downgrade rates and churn risk.
* Premium subscribers show stronger platform loyalty and engagement.

### ⏱️ Content Consumption Insights

* Premium subscribers generate significantly higher watch hours.
* High-engagement users consume content more frequently across multiple devices.
* Consistent content consumption correlates with increased subscription retention.

### 💰 Revenue Intelligence

* Premium plans contribute the largest share of subscription revenue.
* Subscription upgrades are a major driver of revenue growth.
* Retained premium users provide the highest long-term customer value.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 🎯 Improve User Retention

* Develop personalized engagement campaigns for inactive users.
* Introduce targeted reactivation strategies for churn-prone subscribers.
* Enhance customer loyalty programs.

### 🤖 Content Personalization

* Implement AI-powered recommendation systems.
* Deliver personalized content suggestions based on viewing behavior.
* Increase content discoverability through intelligent recommendations.

### 🎥 Content Strategy

* Expand regional and local-language content libraries.
* Invest in high-performing content categories.
* Optimize content acquisition decisions using viewer analytics.

### 📱 Subscription Growth

* Launch telecom and broadband bundle partnerships.
* Offer personalized subscription plans.
* Introduce flexible pricing models for different audience segments.

### 🌍 Market Expansion

* Focus acquisition strategies on Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.
* Create localized marketing campaigns.
* Improve accessibility for emerging user segments.
